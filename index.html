Masashi Kishimoto

Japanese animator, producer, author and manga artist

The Life of Masashi Kishimoto

Mashashi Kishimoto is one of the most successful manga-ka, or manga artists, in the world. His long-running series about ninja-in-training Naruto Uzumaki has sold tens of millions of books around the world.

Kishimoto's works portrays that every aspect of our life. Loneliness, hardship, friends, family bonds makes us stronger in life. Naruto show it all to us.

  • 1978

    Born in Okoyama Prefecture, Japan

  • 1995

    During his last years of college at the Kyushu Sangyo University, Kishimoto spent time drawing manga and went to an art college with hopes that he would become a manga artist.

  • 1995

    Masashi Kishimoto's first work as a manga artist was Karakuri (カラクリ?).

  • 1996

    Karakuri earned him the Weekly Shōnen Jump's monthly "Hop Step Award" in 1996, granted to promising new manga artists.

  • 1997

    In summer 1997 the first one-shot of Naruto by Masashi Kishimoto was published in the Akamaru Jump Summer.

  • 1998

    Masashi Kishimoto premiered as a Weekly Shōnen Jump artist with a serialized version of Karakuri in Weekly Shōnen Jump, but it proved unpopular and was canceled soon after.

  • 1999

    In 1999, a serialized version of Naruto began publication in Weekly Shōnen Jump and quickly became a hit. Naruto is still ongoing, with 60 volumes and 58 released in English, and has sold over 113 million copies in Japan and over 95 million copies were sold in the US followed by over 93 million copies worldwide (outside Japan and United States) as of volume 36, also being adapted into two successful anime series.

  • 2002

    He began to produce movies, shorts and video games of Naruto.

  • 2004

    The release of the first movie of Naruto titled "Naruto the Movie: Ninja Clash in the Land of Snow"

  • 2006

    The Naruto manga series has become one of Viz Media's top properties, accounting for nearly 10% of all manga sales in the US in 2006.The seventh volume of Viz's release became the first manga to ever win a Quill Award when it claimed the award for "Best Graphic Novel" in 2006

  • 2007

    In 2007, the part 2 of Masashi Kishimoto's manga series Naruto Shippudenwas release.

  • 2008

    The release of first Naruto video game "Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Storm"

  • 2009

    In 2009, Masashi Kishimoto began to designed an extra costume for the video game character Lars Alexandersson for Tekken 6.

  • 2014

    After the long run of Naruto, Kishimoto announce that he is supervising Naruto last film The Last: Naruto the Movie, which would act as a bridge connecting the series' conclusion and epilogue.

  • 2017

    Mashashi Kishimoto announce that he already has finalized what he wants to do for his next manga series. A sci-fi manga, the series will feature a unique protagonist. And in 2017, Jump Fiesta confirm it and the manga make a debut in 2018.

  • 2020 - present

    In November 2020, it was announced that after 51 chapters and 13 volumes Kodachi would step down as writer of the Boruto: Naruto Next Generations manga, with Kishimoto assuming full writing duties and Ikemoto continuing as illustrator beginning with chapter 52 in the upcoming December issue of V Jump magazine, published on November 21, 2020.
